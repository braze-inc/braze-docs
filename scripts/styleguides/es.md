# Spanish style guide

## Grammatical gender for "Braze"

Avoid gendered articles directly before "Braze" when possible (prefer "de Braze", "con Braze"). When an article is required, match the gender of the head noun:

- "el Braze SDK" (because "el SDK")

## Terminology

- "Feature Flags" should be translated as **conmutador de características** (plural: conmutadores de características) to match the Braze product UI in Spanish. Do not use "banderas de características".

## Register and tone

- Use formal Spanish (usted) appropriate for technical documentation
- Maintain a professional, instructional tone
