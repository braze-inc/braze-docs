guide_top_text: "Attributes in Braze describe who your users are. Standard attributes are predefined profile fields—like first name, email, phone number, date of birth, country, and language—that are supported across Braze SDKs and set using predefined methods or APIs. Custom attributes are brand-specific, flexible fields you define to capture traits unique to your business, such as subscription tier or favorite category. Together, they power segmentation, personalization, and analytics across your campaigns and Canvases."
