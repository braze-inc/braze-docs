# Canva

## About the integration

The Braze and Canva integration allows you to push assets from Canva directly into the Braze Media library.

## Integration

### Step 1: Install the Braze App in Canva

The Braze app can be found in the [Canva Apps Marketplace](#todo).

After installing the app it will be available within a design, under the Apps menu.

![An image of the Braze app in Canva]({% image_buster /assets/img/canva_integration/braze-canva-app.png %})

### Step 2: Authorize your Braze account

Launch the Braze app, and click Connect to begin the authorization flow. This will grant Canva access to list the Braze workspaces you have access to, as well as permission to create new media library assets on your behalf.

![An image of the authorization flow for granting Canva access to your Braze account]({% image_buster /assets/img/canva_integration/canva-oauth.jpg %})

### Step 3: Export the Image to Braze

When you're ready to export your Canva design to Braze, choose your desired destination workspace, optionally provide a filename, and click **Start Export**.

![An image of the Canva upload to Braze flow]({% image_buster /assets/img/canva_integration/canva-upload-screen.jpg %})

### Step 4: Result

Once your export completes, your new asset will be available in the Media Library, with a source of "Canva".

![A media library asset showing a source of Canva in Braze]({% image_buster /assets/img/canva_integration/media-library-source.jpg %})
